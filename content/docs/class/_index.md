title: Classes
---

Barbarian: Storm with Rage, and wade into hand-to-hand combat.

Bard: Perform spells that inspire and heal allies or beguile foes.

Cleric: Invoke divine magic to heal, bolster, and smite. 

Druid: Channel nature magic to heal, shape-shift, and control the elements. 

Fighter: Master all weapons and armor.

Monk: Dart in and out of melee while striking fast and hard. 

Paladin: Smite foes and shield allies with divine and martial might. 

Ranger: Weave together martial prowess, nature magic, and survival skills.

Rogue: Launch deadly Sneak Attacks while avoiding harm through stealth. 

Sorcerer: Wield magic innate to your being, shaping the power to your will. 

Warlock: Cast spells derived from occult knowledge. 

Wizard: Study arcane magic and master spells for every purpose.
